Additional Information

BrewForge is for people who enjoy playing tabletop role-playing games such as Dungeons and Dragons, Pathfinder, Fantasy Flight Star Wars, and many more. Here you can access and download adventure modules, full campaigns, and an array of quality role-playing content that others have created and shared. Do all of this for free, or, with a $5 monthly subscription, remove ads from your browsing experience, get access to additional content, such as premium adventure modules, and help grow BrewForge.

In addition to being able to access and download content, you can share your own role-playing creations, adventures, maps, characters etc. The world will be able to see, use, and endorse your creations. With a Game Master membership of $15 dollars you gain all the benefits of a base BrewForge membership and are able to host all of your content on BrewForge servers. With Game Master privileges you are also eligible to receive donations from users who endorse your content (with a small portion of the donation going to the site for hosting content).

Find what content you are looking for by searching for the game you play, the content-type, etc. Enhance your role-playing sessions with quality creations from other users, or get the recognition you always wanted for your amazing adventures that deserve to be shared and experienced by others around the world.

A better way to role-play, BrewForge.
